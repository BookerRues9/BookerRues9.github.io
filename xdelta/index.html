XDeltaTool Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application XDeltaTool, developed and published by Booker Dev.

General Information

XDeltaTool is a free Android application that displays ads using Google AdMob and integrates Google Play Billing to offer a Pro mode. The Pro version removes ads and usage limitations.

Data Collection

XDeltaTool itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data. AdMob may use device identifiers for delivering ads according to Google’s privacy policies. The app does not access, process, or transmit any user information.

User Content

The app does not distribute or include any external files. All patches and related content are provided, shared, and managed entirely by the user. This means that all content processed within the app depends solely on the user’s own files.

Permissions

XDeltaTool may request access to the device’s storage using the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF) to read and write patch-related files. This permission is used strictly for the app’s core functionality and does not involve external data transmission.

Pro Mode

The Pro mode is an optional in-app purchase that removes ads and usage limitations. All purchases are handled securely through Google Play Billing in accordance with Google Play policies.

Security and Network

The app works primarily offline and does not require an internet connection, except for loading ads or processing in-app purchases through Google Play services.
