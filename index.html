QuickTale Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application QuickTale, developed and published by Booker Dev.

QuickTale uses third-party services to provide gameplay enhancements and monetization options. These include:

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect data such as device identifiers, usage data, and interaction data to show personalized or non-personalized ads in accordance with their privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Google Play Billing is used to offer an optional in-app purchase to remove ads. All purchases are handled securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Google Play Games

Google Play Games Services is used for achievements and leaderboards. Player ID, name, and avatar may be accessed by Google, according to their privacy policies.

Data Storage

No personal user data is collected, stored, or shared by QuickTale. Local storage is only used for saving game progress or loading local content.

Cookies and Analytics

This app does not use cookies or third-party analytics tools.
