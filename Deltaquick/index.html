DeltaQuick

A simple launcher for running all available chapters of DT on Android.

What is DeltaQuick?

DeltaQuick is a lightweight and clean launcher that lets you run chapters 1 to 4 of DT from one place. The app does not include any game files—you must legally own them through your PC version.

Main Features

  • Launch DT chapters 1–4 with ease
  • Offline support after setup
  • No ads, tracking, or extra permissions
  • Automatic updates via Google Play

How to Use

  1. Transfer your DT files from your PC (Steam version is required)
  2. Place them in the appropriate folder as described inside the app
  3. Open DeltaQuick and launch your preferred chapter

Privacy Policy

DeltaQuick does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline, except when checking for updates through Google Play.
